Optimal Weight Finder

The Amazing Space Weight Loss/Gain Program

Are you having trouble getting to your ideal "summer weight"? Whether you would like to
be lighter or heavier, fear not, the Amazing Space Weight Loss/Gain Program has a
solution -- just relocate to a planet where you'll be that ideal weight.

Instructions:

1. Enter your name, Weight on Earth, and the weight you would like to be.
2. Enter potential planets -- one or more of (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter,
Saturn, Uranus, Neptune) separated by spaces -- where you would be willing to
relocate to.
3. Click on the button to find the optimal planet, from among those you listed, where
you would weight closest to your desired weight.
